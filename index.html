What Is This?

Introducing TeamCDC's Time Management App for Kids, designed to help children develop essential organizational skills in a fun and engaging way. Our app empowers kids to manage their daily tasks and activities, fostering responsibility and independence from an early age.

What is Priority (1-10) Explanation

You can give 5 to 10 transits to the subject you want to prioritize and schedule more time for it.

You can study less and give a lower priority to the subject, that is between 1 and 5 and schedule the time according to the number you have given.
